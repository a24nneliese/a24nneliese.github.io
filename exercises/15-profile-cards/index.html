Jon Snow

Known for his strong sense of honor, loyalty, and courage. He is humble and often puts duty above personal desire. Jon values fairness and justice, even when it costs him personally. Though quiet and serious, he is compassionate and willing to sacrifice himself to protect others.

Sansa

Intelligent, resilient, and patient. She grows from being naive and idealistic into a strong, strategic, and emotionally tough leader. Sansa learns from hardship, becoming cautious, wise, and determined to protect her family and her people.

Bran

Quiet, thoughtful, and deeply intuitive. After his transformation into the Three-Eyed Raven, he becomes wise, calm, and detached, guided by knowledge rather than emotion. Bran values understanding and long-term vision over personal ambition.
