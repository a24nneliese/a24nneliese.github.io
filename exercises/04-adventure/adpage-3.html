Winterfell

Winterfell is the ancestral seat of the North, a place of duty, honor, and legacy. Jon Snow is deeply tied to it, embodying loyalty, courage, and a strong moral compass. His role is central as a leader and protector, often carrying the burdens of others, and his character is defined by integrity, selflessness, and a willingness to face danger for the greater good.

You must choose to kneel to Jon Snow or betray him for Queen Cersei.
