My Movie Showcase

Parasite

GENRE Thriller/Comedy RUNTIME 2h 12m   RATING

Bong Joon-ho’s Parasite (2019) is a dark thriller-comedy exploring class conflict, where the struggling Kim family infiltrates the wealthy Park household by posing as highly qualified, unrelated employees. Their scheme to escape poverty escalates when they discover a hidden, dark secret in the Park’s basement, triggering a violent, unexpected chain of events.

Portrait of A Lady On Fire

GENRE Drama/Romance   RUNTIME 2h 1m   RATING

Portrait of a Lady on Fire (2019) is a French historical drama by Céline Sciamma about Marianne, a painter commissioned to secretly paint a portrait of Héloïse, a reluctant bride-to-be on an isolated Breton island. The two women develop an intense, forbidden romance while navigating themes of the female gaze, memory, and societal constraints.

Girl, interrupted

GENRE Drama   RUNTIME 2h 7m   RATING

Girl, Interrupted (1999) is a drama based on Susanna Kaysen's memoir about her 18-month stay at a 1960s psychiatric hospital after a suicide attempt. Diagnosed with Borderline Personality Disorder, she navigates the institution, befriending troubled patients—notably the manipulative, charismatic Lisa Rowe—while questioning the blurred lines between sanity and insanity, ultimately finding her path to recovery.

Fight Club

GENRE Drama   RUNTIME 2h 19m   RATING

Fight Club (1999) is a cult classic thriller about a nameless, insomniac office worker (Edward Norton) who creates a chaotic alter ego, Tyler Durden (Brad Pitt), to escape his shallow, consumerist life. They start an underground, violent fighting ring that evolves into a cult-like anarchic organization, Project Mayhem. The story explores themes of repressed masculinity, existential dread, and the shadow self.
